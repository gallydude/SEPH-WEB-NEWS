Executive Summary

The latest SEPH Labour Market Intelligence Newsletter highlights significant employment events across various sectors in Canada. Notable negative impacts are expected in the mining (NAICS 21) and manufacturing (NAICS 31-33) sectors, primarily due to layoffs and restructuring at companies such as Rio Tinto, Nike, Polaris, and Algoma Steel. Conversely, the energy and utilities sector (NAICS 22) is anticipated to experience a substantial positive impact with the announcement of 18,900 new nuclear jobs at Bruce Power. However, this sector is also facing negative pressures from ongoing strikes at Hydro-Québec, which may lead to project delays and job losses.
