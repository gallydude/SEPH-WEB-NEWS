Reference Month: 2026-06  |  Issued: July 01, 2026

Executive Summary

This month's SEPH Labour Market Intelligence Newsletter highlights significant employment events across various sectors. Notable negative impacts are expected in the transportation sector (NAICS 48-49), particularly in the rail industry, due to multiple strikes and labour disputes, including those involving Canadian Pacific-Kansas City and Canadian National Railway. Conversely, the retail and manufacturing sectors (NAICS 31-33) are likely to experience unusual movement, with both positive and negative events, such as the hiring of 10,000 local employees by Tim Hortons and layoffs at Volkswagen, Rivian, and Algoma Steel. Additionally, the expansion of Siemens Canada and the return of Zellers are expected to have a positive impact on employment in the manufacturing and retail sectors, respectively.
